### Artist Statement（后续页面可能需要）

```
I am DCT (Mengzhen Yang), a Chinese visual artist born in 1993 in Ma'anshan, China. For over a decade, I have worked exclusively by hand — oil on canvas, charcoal, watercolor — creating large-scale, physically intensive paintings built through palette knife and impasto techniques over months and years. My painting "I Am a Growth" took over three years to complete.

Most of my paintings originate from dreams. They carry the weight of unconscious imagery translated through physical labor into paint. Each surface is not merely painted but sculpted, scraped, and accumulated — carrying the memory of every session.

Recently, I have begun exploring the intersection of traditional hand-painting and digital media — merging charcoal portraiture with digital disruption, and translating painterly gesture into generative code. This new direction allows me to examine how bodily energy can be encoded into algorithmic processes while preserving the rawness of handmade art.
```

### Residency Proposal（驻留计划）

```
Project Title: Between Dream and Material — Large-Scale Oil Painting Series

During my residency at Serlachius, I plan to develop a new series of large-scale oil paintings inspired by the Finnish landscape, light conditions, and the dialogue between nature and industrial heritage that defines Mänttä.

My working process is deeply physical — I use palette knives and impasto techniques to build thick, sculptural surfaces over extended periods. The 1-4 month residency format aligns perfectly with my practice, as my paintings require sustained, uninterrupted time to develop.

I am particularly drawn to Serlachius because of its unique setting — the intersection of world-class art collections (Serlachius Museums) and the quiet, focused environment of a small Finnish art town. This combination of artistic stimulation and contemplative solitude is exactly what my practice requires.

During the residency, I would welcome the opportunity to organize a small exhibition or open studio event in the gallery space, sharing my working process and completed paintings with the local community.

Deliverables:
- 2-4 new large-scale oil paintings
- Written report with documentation of process and work
- Optional: exhibition or open studio event
```

### Portfolio Description（作品描述）

```
1. "Jungle Descent" (2026) — Oil on canvas
   Dense tropical composition built through heavy impasto, exploring the boundary between natural chaos and painterly control.

2. "Crimson Sovereign" (2026) — Oil on canvas, 120×180cm
   A monumental figure study in deep crimson, featuring a fox rendered through thick palette knife strokes.

3. "I Am a Growth" (2015-2019) — Oil on canvas, 26×24 in
   Three-year painting exploring organic growth through accumulated layers of paint. Each session left a geological record in the surface.

4. "Achilla" (2017) — Oil on canvas, 36×24 in
   Classical portrait rendered through contemporary impasto technique.

5. "Portrait Glitch" series (2025-2026) — Charcoal + digital
   Hand-drawn charcoal portraits progressively disrupted by digital overlays, exploring identity fragmentation.
```
