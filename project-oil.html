Impasto Oil Paintings

Large-scale oil paintings born from dreams. Built through palette knife and impasto over months and years. Paint is not applied — it is sculpted, scraped, accumulated. Each surface carries the physical memory of every session.

来自梦境的大尺幅油画。用刮刀和厚涂技法，历经数月甚至数年完成。颜料不是涂上去的——是雕刻、刮削、堆积而成。每一层表面都承载着每次创作的身体记忆。
