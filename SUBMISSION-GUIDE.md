### Brief / Background（简报/背景，300字以内）
```
Chronicles of the Mind is a self-initiated cross-medium art project by Mengzhen Yang, proposing a post-humanist framework for contemporary art practice. The project spans physical oil painting, digital illustration, generative algorithmic art, and pixel art — unified by a singular artistic vision that refuses the partition between artist and engineer, between canvas and laboratory.

The Genesis Pitch is an institutional takeover proposal: a manifesto and portfolio demanding that art institutions provide laboratory access, expert collaboration, and R&D funding to realize artworks that merge physical media with computational systems. The project challenges the traditional gallery-industrial complex by positioning the artist not as supplicant but as architect of new institutional models.
```

### Solution / Idea Description（解决方案/创意描述，300字以内）
```
The work centers on the "Crimson Sovereign" series — monumental oil paintings of foxes rendered in violent impasto technique, where paint is sculpted rather than applied. These physical works are paired with their digital counterparts: a crowned astral figure in constellation robes, generative art pieces that translate painterly gesture into code, and pixel art that compresses consciousness to its most essential resolution.

The complete project is presented as a 16-page institutional pitch deck and a live web exhibition (realid-zz.github.io/pencil), designed with extreme minimalist typography in the tradition of high-fashion editorial design. The "Death Pencil" aesthetic — raw, dark, uncompromising — runs through every element, from the impasto knife-work to the cross-hatched generative algorithms.

The work demands to be encountered, not merely viewed.
```
