I paint because language fails.

Born 1993, Ma'anshan, China. I work in oil on canvas — large-scale, physically intensive works built through palette knife and impasto over months and years. My painting "I Am a Growth" took over three years.

My practice spans oil painting, charcoal, watercolor, digital illustration, and generative code — unified by what I call Death Pencil: raw, confrontational, rooted in the tension between control and chaos.
