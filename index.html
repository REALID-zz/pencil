About the Artist

I paint because language fails.

Born 1993 in Ma'anshan, China. I work primarily in oil on canvas — large-scale, physically intensive works built through aggressive impasto and palette-knife technique. My painting I Am a Growth took over three years of continuous creation.

My practice spans oil painting, charcoal drawing, watercolor, digital illustration, and generative code — unified by an uncompromising aesthetic I call Death Pencil: raw, confrontational, rooted in the tension between control and chaos.

I do not sketch first. I begin with color and force. The image reveals itself through accumulation — layer over layer, session over session, sometimes over years.
