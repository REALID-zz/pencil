Most of my paintings come from dreams.

Born 1993, Ma'anshan, China. Studied fine art at Gwangju University, South Korea. Spent nearly a decade in the United States building a professional practice across Los Angeles and New York.

For over a decade I have worked exclusively by hand — oil on canvas, charcoal, watercolor — creating large-scale, physically intensive paintings through palette knife and impasto. My painting "I Am a Growth" took over three years. Recently I began exploring traditional painting and digital media.

我的大多数画都来自于梦境。在韩国光州大学学习美术后，赴美近十年，先后在洛杉矶和纽约进行职业艺术创作。2020年获全美杰出华人30 Under 30，获美国国会嘉奖。作品曾在波士顿、纽约世贸中心、百老汇等地展出。十余年坚持纯手绘——油画、炭笔、水彩。最近开始研究传统手绘与数字媒体艺术的结合。
